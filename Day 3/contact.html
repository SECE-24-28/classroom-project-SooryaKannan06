Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for recharge to reflect? +
Most recharges are processed instantly. In rare cases, it may take up to 5-10 minutes due to operator delays.
What should I do if my recharge fails? +
If your recharge fails, the amount will be automatically refunded to your payment method within 3-5 business days. You can also contact our support team for immediate assistance.
Can I get a refund for a successful recharge? +
Refunds for successful recharges are not possible as the amount is directly credited to your mobile account. Please double-check the mobile number before confirming the recharge.
Is my payment information secure? +
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways. Your payment information is never stored on our servers and is processed through certified payment partners.
Do you offer cashback on recharges? +
Yes, we regularly offer cashback and promotional discounts on recharges. Check our offers section or subscribe to notifications to stay updated on the latest deals.
